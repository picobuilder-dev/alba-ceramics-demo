About Alba Ceramics

Alba Ceramics is a South African manufacturer specialising in the production of high-quality ceramic vases and decorative pottery

With over 25 years of experience, our skilled team manages the full production process — from raw clay preparation to moulding, kiln firing, and final finishing.

We supply retailers and businesses nationwide, offering consistent quality, competitive pricing, and the ability to create custom designs tailored to client needs.
